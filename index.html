Major Power Outage Analysis Project

Project Overview

This project analyzes major power outages in the United States, exploring patterns in location, timing, and severity to understand risk factors and predict outage characteristics.

Research Questions

  • Where and when do major power outages tend to occur?
  • What are the characteristics of major power outages with higher severity?
  • How do weather conditions impact outage severity?
